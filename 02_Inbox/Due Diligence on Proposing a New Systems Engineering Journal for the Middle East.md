Due Diligence on Proposing a New Systems Engineering Journal for the Middle East

# Options

A few options, in increasing difficulty:

1. Special issue / section in an existing IEEE or ACM journal on “Systems Engineering in the Middle East.”  
      
    
2. Proceedings-style series (e.g., an IEEE/ACM co-sponsored annual conference in SE, with proceedings in Xplore / DL).  
      
    
3. A new journal / transactions focused on Systems Engineering (with “Middle East” angle in scope).
    

Path 1 or 2 is usually the on-ramp to 3. IEEE/ACM are far more willing to start a journal when there is already a track / series with proven submissions and citations.

# Pitching the Story

Both IEEE and ACM will care less about the geography and more about:

- Unmet need:  
      
    

- Demonstrate that existing SE/MBSE journals are overloaded or don’t cover:  
      
    

- Infrastructure, defense, space, and mega-projects specific to MENA.  
      
    
- Policy, regulatory, and socio-technical contexts specific to the region.  
      
    

- Critical mass:  
      
    

- Show there are enough researchers and practitioners in the region who:  
      
    

- Publish in SE/MBSE/RE/concurrent engineering, etc.  
      
    
- Would move good papers to this journal.  
      
    

- Sustainability:  
      
    

- Tie to:  
      
    

- Existing conferences (INCOSE MENA, local SE conferences).  
      
    
- Regional centers of excellence (labs, universities, industry).  
      
    
- Government/industry partners (sponsors, invited special issues).  
      
    

Practically, the “Middle East” angle is best expressed as:

- A key part of the scope and positioning, not necessarily the journal title.  
      
    
- A strong representation in the editorial board and case studies.
    

# Ideal Roadmap

### Phase 0 – Reality check with IEEE & ACM

- Reach out to:  
      
    

- IEEE Systems Council / SMC Society VP–Publications or equivalent.  
      
    
- ACM’s Publications contact for new journals plus relevant SIG chairs. [ACM+1](https://www.acm.org/publications/start-a-new-acm-publication?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
    

- We Ask explicitly:  
      
    

- “Is a new systems engineering journal plausible in the next 3–5 years?”  
      
    
- “Would you prefer an ACM PACM-style proceedings journal, a co-sponsored journal with IEEE, or a series of special issues first?”  
      
    

That conversation will tell us which lane is realistic.

### Phase 1 – Build evidence and community (1–2 years)

- Run:  
      
    

- A recurring workshop or conference on Systems Engineering in Complex MENA Projects.  
      
    
- One or more special issues in existing IEEE/ACM journals, co-guest-edited by you and senior people.  
      
    

- Collect:  
      
    

- Submission/acceptance numbers.  
      
    

Download/citation stats.
