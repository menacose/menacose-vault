We are proud to announce [Dr.-Ing. Bassem Hassan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/drbassemhassan/) as the Vice President of the INCOSE MENA chapter and its EGYCOSE sub-chapter — a strategic leader in systems engineering and transportation innovation.  
  
Dr. Hassan is the Transportation & Mobility Industry Director at Dassault Systèmes. He brings over 20 years in automotive industry with wide experience in systems engineering, model-based systems engineering (MBSE), and digital transformation. His career includes leadership roles at AUDI AG, CARIAD (Volkswagen Group), and Fraunhofer IEM.  
  
He has led major programs in ADAS, automated driving, and virtual twin technology, shaping the systems engineering landscape across Europe and the Middle East.  
  
He is a recognized voice in the field — keynote speaker, published author, and advisor — with a strong focus on mentoring, education, and building bridges between industry, academia, and public institutions.  
  
His deep expertise in systems thinking, architecture, and digital continuity will be instrumental in strengthening the INCOSE Egypt Chapter and expanding its global relevance.  
  
🔗Follow his work: [www.bassem.eu](http://www.bassem.eu/)  
  
We are proud to have him lead our mission to advance systems engineering in Egypt and across the region.
