
## Organisation

01_Governance: For the governance topics of our chapters: By-Laws, Vision, Mission, Strategy, etc.

02_Inbox: For any new item that needs to be discussed/processed by us like an inquiry to reply to or a proposal to study and decide on it. Consider it like our mailbox / incoming requests filtering space.

03_Meetings: For notes about any meetings we have whether board meetings or other meeting.

04_Project-AND-Initiatives: A space for our knowledge base for all information related to projects and initiatives' we have.

05_ to 09_: Spaces for chairs to manage their assets and knowledge base.

90_Attachments: For holding all attachments: images, screenshots, and PDFs.

99_Tempaltes: For templates. To have a database of all of our asset. We will use light templates including metadata like: Date of creation. status, tags/hashtags, etc.

## Processes

1. You select the relevant space to create your note.
2. Check if there is a suitable template to use, if not create a new one following the pattern of others. If your note content or metadata needs will not be recurring, DON'T create a template.
3. Try to use backlinks and tags where suitable/necessary to have connected/linked notes.
4. To draw something, select where you would like to draw it, right-click and select "New drawing".
5. You can use the toolbar in the top for formatting like in Word.
6. If you want to add/take a slot of the bi-weekly Qahwa or Expert Talk, go to the Advanced Agenda to add your desired event.
7. Don't install or use additional community plugins.
